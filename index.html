About Me

DefendHer is a personal safety app designed to empower women with a sense of security and confidence. With features like panic alerts, GPS tracking, and emergency contact notifications, DefendHer provides a safe and reliable way for women to navigate their daily lives, ensuring their safety and well-being at all times.
